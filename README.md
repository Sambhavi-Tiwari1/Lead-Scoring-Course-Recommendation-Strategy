# Lead Scoring & Course Recommendation Strategy

**Project Duration:** Dec 2024 – May 2025  
**Type:** Self Project / Data Science Portfolio  
**Status:** Completed

## 📌 Project Overview

In the competitive EdTech landscape, converting a lead into a paying customer requires more than just a good product; it requires understanding the nuanced drivers of consumer behavior. This project focuses on building a data-driven framework to prioritize leads and recommend personalized course offerings.

By analyzing behavioral, demographic, and social factors, this strategy aims to bridge the gap between user interest and enrollment, ultimately enhancing marketing efficiency and boosting conversion rates.

## 🎯 Objectives

1.  **Optimize Conversion:** Identify the key factors that influence a customer's decision to purchase a course.
2.  **Prioritize Leads:** Develop a robust scoring system to rank leads based on their likelihood to convert, allowing sales teams to focus on high-value prospects.
3.  **Leverage Social Proof:** Quantify the impact of ratings, reviews, and social signals on user purchasing behavior.
4.  **Enhance Personalization:** Segment users effectively to deliver tailored course recommendations that match their specific needs and skill levels.
5.  **Increase ROI:** Provide actionable recommendations to improve marketing spend efficiency and boost enrollment numbers.

## 🛠️ Project Workflow & Methodology

### 1. Data Analysis & Factor Evaluation
- **Objective:** Understand what drives purchases.
- **Action:** Analyzed historical user data to evaluate the correlation between different variables (price, duration, difficulty) and final purchase decisions.
- **Key Finding:** Identified that specific review sentiments have a higher impact on conversion than star ratings alone.

### 2. Social Proof Assessment
- **Objective:** Measure the influence of reviews and ratings.
- **Action:** Conducted sentiment analysis on textual reviews and combined it with quantitative rating data.
- **Key Finding:** Quantified how an increase in positive social proof correlates with a higher probability of lead conversion.

### 3. User Segmentation
- **Objective:** Group users for targeted strategies.
- **Action:** Implemented clustering algorithms (e.g., RFM Analysis / K-Means) based on behavioral data (browsing history, time spent) and demographic data.
- **Key Finding:** Established distinct user personas (e.g., "Career Switchers," "Skill Enhancers," "Hobbyists") with unique buying patterns.

### 4. Recommendation Framework Design
- **Objective:** Align courses with user needs.
- **Action:** Built a recommendation engine logic that pairs user segments with the most suitable course catalog based on skill gaps and learning goals.

### 5. Strategic Recommendations
- **Objective:** Drive business growth.
- **Action:** Synthesized analytical findings into strategic business recommendations for marketing, sales, and product teams.
